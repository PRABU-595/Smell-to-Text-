\begin{abstract}
We present the first neural approach to translate natural language smell 
descriptions into chemical formulas. Using fine-tuned NeoBERT on a novel 
dataset of 5,000 perfume descriptions paired with chemical compositions, 
we achieve X\% MAP and Y\% P@5, outperforming BERT baselines by Z\%. 
This work enables rapid prototyping in the perfume industry and opens 
new directions for cross-modal sensory AI.
\end{abstract}
